# ITSAC 2027 · Logo colour usage rules (canonical)

Derived from the full logo-colours × 8 backgrounds decision pass (21 July 2026), revised 22 July 2026: the cream-only coloured accent was removed. These are the source of truth for which logo colour may sit on which background. A colour decision applies to all three layouts (landscape, portrait, square); the rule is about colour, not shape.

## The two rules

1. **The mark is only ever black or white.** No coloured versions of the mark are used, on any background. Gold and grey are likewise never used as the mark colour.
2. **Mark colour follows background lightness.** Use the **black** mark on light backgrounds (White, Cream, Gold). Use the **white** mark on dark or vivid backgrounds (Ink, Blue, Green, Terracotta, Grey).

Everything else (same-colour pairings, low-contrast pairings, coloured marks) is disallowed, which the matrix below reflects.
