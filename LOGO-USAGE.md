# ITSAC 2027 · Logo colour usage rules (canonical)

Derived from the full 7 logo-colours × 8 backgrounds decision pass, 21 July 2026. These are the source of truth for which logo colour may sit on which background. A colour decision applies to all three layouts (landscape, portrait, square), the rule is about colour, not shape.

## The three rules

1. **Mark colour follows background lightness.** Use the **black** mark on light backgrounds (White, Cream, Gold). Use the **white** mark on dark or vivid backgrounds (Ink, Blue, Green, Terracotta, Grey).
2. **Coloured marks are a cream-only accent.** On the **Cream** background only, the mark may also be **Blue, Green or Terracotta**. On every other background the mark stays black or white per rule 1.
3. **Gold and Grey are never used as the mark colour**, on any background.

Everything else (same-colour pairings, low-contrast pairings) is disallowed, which the matrix below reflects.
